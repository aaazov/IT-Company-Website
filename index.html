FAQ

+

What makes VitaRay different?

VitaRay is completely contactless — it uses radar, not wearables or cameras. Our system uses mmWave radar and AI to monitor vital signs and detect risks like falls or cognitive decline, while preserving the user’s comfort and privacy.

+

Is VitaRay suitable for use at home?

Yes. VitaRay is designed for both private homes and aged care facilities. It’s easy to install, requires no ongoing input from the user, and works in common living areas like bedrooms, bathrooms, and hallways. Family members can receive real-time alerts and insights through a connected platform.

+

How is user privacy protected?

VitaRay does not use cameras or audio — only radar-based sensing. All data is processed with strict privacy protocols, and no personal or visual information is captured. The system is designed to provide peace of mind without compromising dignity.

+

What kind of setup is required?

VitaRay is a plug-and-play system that can be installed by trained staff or facility teams. We provide onboarding support, configuration guidance, and integration with care teams or alert platforms if needed. Ongoing technical support is also available.

+

Can we join your pilot program or clinical trial?

Yes — we are currently designing our clinical study and welcoming expressions of interest from individuals, families, and aged care providers. Please contact us to learn more about participation options.
